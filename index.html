MetaCyc MetaCyc has both a primary and a secondary metabolism pathway,as well as a related metabolite, reaction, enzyme and genetic function. The MetaCyc purpose is to index the metabolism world by preserving a representative sample of each pathway which is experimentally explained. Search for pathways,enzymes, reactions,metabolites Online metabolism encyclopaedia Predict sequenced gene metabolic pathways Supporting enzyme database metabolic engineering Metabolite database facilitates studies into metabolomics MetaCyc contains 2859 pathways from 3185 different organisms https://metacyc.org/ MetaCycUserGuide.shtml MetaCyc data can be accessed in several ways: Search for pathways, enzymes, reactions, and metabolites through MetaCyc.org Download MetaCyc data files Browse a list of all MetaCyc metabolic pathways Add MetaCyc to your browser search bar for instant MetaCyc searching https:// metacyc.org/
BIGG Model BiGG Models is a knowledgebase of GEMs, it combines a collection of starred identifiers known as BiGG IDs into a single pool of over 70 publicated metabolic genome-scale networks. The BiGG model genes are mapped to the NCBI annotations, and various external databases are linked to metabolites (KEGG, PubChem, and many more). searching for a metabolite, reaction, gene, or organism in the search box, browse the content of genome-scale models.
